# Pantry Dates Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Pantry Dates is designed to keep your data on your device.

## Data Collection

Pantry Dates does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the
developer.

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs,
or developer-operated servers.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Pantry Dates stores the information you enter into the app locally on your
device, including:

- Food item names
- Expiration dates
- Notes
- Refrigerated or pantry status
- Flagged status
- Finished-item history
- Selected food icons

This data is stored in the app's local SQLite database.

## Icon Suggestions

Pantry Dates can use Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to suggest a
food icon from the item name you enter. These suggestions are used only to pick a
matching in-app icon. The developer does not receive your item names or icon
suggestion inputs.

## Exporting Data

Pantry Dates includes an optional database export feature. If you choose to
export or share that file, you control where it is sent. The developer does not
receive exported files unless you explicitly send one for support.

## iCloud and Backups

Pantry Dates does not use a developer-operated cloud sync service. Your device
settings may include app data in Apple-managed device backups according to your
iOS backup settings.
